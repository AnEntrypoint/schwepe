## Overview

The Schwepe Lore Library is an interactive, immersive experience exploring the chaotic world of 247420 culture through the lens of our favorite amphibian friend. This is not just a collection of stories—it's a living document that captures the zeitgeist of degen culture, memetic warfare, and the beautiful tragedy of crypto trading.

## Features

- **Age Verification**: Adult content warning with blur overlay (Schwepe pink enter button)
- **Dynamic Lore System**: 9 chapters of interactive content with themes ranging from cyberpunk to cosmic horror
- **Responsive Design**: Glassmorphism effects, animated backgrounds, and mobile-friendly interface
- **Editor Comments**: Cognitive dissonance throughout the text with editorial asides
- **Quantum Frog Mechanics**: Explore the paradoxes of crypto trading through absurdist humor
